Privacy Policy

The Keepit Roulette · Last updated 1 July 2026

The Keepit Roulette ("the app") is a Microsoft Teams meeting app that lets participants randomly select someone using an interactive spinning wheel. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

What the app accesses

When you add the app to a Teams meeting, it reads the following information through the Microsoft Teams client, solely to build and display the wheel:

  • The display names of meeting participants.
  • Basic meeting context provided by Teams (such as the meeting identifier and your Teams theme).

How the data is used

Participant names are used only to populate the wheel and to announce the selected winner during the meeting. The data is processed in your browser for the duration of the session.

What the app does not do

  • It does not store participant data on any server.
  • It does not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.
  • It does not use your data for advertising or profiling.
  • It does not track you across meetings or other applications.

Data storage and retention

The app is a static web application. Meeting and participant information is held in memory only while the app is open and is discarded when you close the meeting tab or panel. No personal data is retained after the session ends.

Hosting

The app is hosted as static content on GitHub Pages. Standard web server logs (such as IP address and request time) may be collected by the hosting provider as part of normal operation. See GitHub's privacy statement for details.

Your choices

Because no personal data is stored, there is nothing to delete or export. You can stop all data processing at any time by removing the app from the meeting or closing the app panel.
